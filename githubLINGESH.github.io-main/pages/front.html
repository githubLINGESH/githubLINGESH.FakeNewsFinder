About Fake News and Fake Video Detection

Welcome to our website dedicated to combating misinformation and fake content online. With the rise of digital media, it has become increasingly challenging to distinguish between genuine news and fabricated stories, as well as authentic videos and manipulated footage. Our platform aims to address this issue by providing powerful tools for detecting and identifying fake news and videos.
